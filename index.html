How to Get Your Strike CSV Report

  1. Login to https://dashboard.strike.me/
  2. Click "Activity" on the left sidebar
  3. Click "Generate report" button
  4. Choose "All transactions" in the dropdown
  5. Select "BTC" for the asset
  6. Enter your desired date range
  7. Click "Download"
  8. Upload the downloaded CSV file to this site for performance analysis
